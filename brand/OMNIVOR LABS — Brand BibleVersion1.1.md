**2. Three Pillars Messaging Framework (1-2 hours)**
Develop the V-O-R pillars as a value prop structure:

| Pillar | Capability | Module Mapping | One-liner |
|--------|------------|----------------|-----------|
| **Virtualization** | Digital twins, simulations, scenario modeling | Scenario Lab | "Project outcomes before you commit resources." |
| **Orchestration** | Campaign coordination, constraint-aware optimization, agent systems | Catalyst Queue, Portfolio Optimizer | "Coordinate complexity across every channel." |
| **Reporting** | Dashboards, diagnostics, insights, exports | Nerve Center, Signal Diagnostic | "See what's working. Know what's not." |

**3. Wordmark Variant Consideration (Design decision)**
Two options:
```
Option A: Subtle color split
Omni (white) VOR (purple) — VOR in accent color

Option B: Weight differentiation  
Omni (700) VOR (900) — VOR heavier

Option C: Keep uniform, let context explain
OMNIVOR LABS — introduce acronym in copy, not typography
```

I'd lean **Option C** for the wordmark itself (cleaner), but use the V-O-R breakdown in marketing materials, pitch decks, and the "About" section.
